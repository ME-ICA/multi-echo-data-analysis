Recommended Reading#

Journal articles describing multi-echo methods#

Posse [2012] includes an historical overview of multi-echo acquisition and research. Kundu et al. [2017] is a review of multi-echo denoising with a focus on the MEICA algorithm. The appendix of Olafsson et al. [2015] includes a good explanation of the math underlying MEICA denoising. The appendix of Dipasquale et al. [2017] includes some recommendations for multi-echo acquisition.
